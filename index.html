about

It is said that black consumers in the are worth Â£300bn to the UK economy yet have very little traction when it comes to others purchasing from their own businesses. They also face obstacles like investment funding. The 'Black Pound Day' started by music artist Swiss which commenced on 27th June 2020, which is a monthly initiative to celebrate, support and create awareness of black owned businesses in the UK. It is to challenge everyone's everyday spending decisions and in turn contribute to the UK black-owned business. At Market Black London, our aim is to keep that momentum going not just one day a month but everyday, and why not start at the country's capital: London.

It has proven difficult to locate and access black-owned businesses so Market Black London was birthed to make it easier. Scroll down to see our initial recommendations, what they have to offer and maybe pay them a visit one day!
